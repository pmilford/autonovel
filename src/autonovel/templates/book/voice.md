## Part 3 — Custom rubric

<!--
Optional. Bullet items here are scored AND flagged by /autonovel:evaluate
and /autonovel:reader-panel on every chapter, in addition to the standard
rubric. Use for book-specific writing rules that don't belong in the
shared command files.

Each bullet is one criterion. Phrase as a rule, with a numeric or
binary check the judge can apply. Examples:

- At most 2 explicit financial transactions per chapter (a number,
  currency, or sum changing hands on-page). Compress excess to
  consequence (whose trust broke, what the silence after the count
  meant), not arithmetic.
- At most 1 dream sequence in the whole book; flag any chapter that
  uses one as scene-padding rather than character revelation.
- Every chapter ending must commit to one specific irreversible
  thing the protagonist can't take back; flag chapters whose final
  scene resets to the opening status quo.

Leave empty (or delete this whole section) if you have no per-book
rules — the standard rubric still applies.

## Part 4 — Per-character voice fingerprints
